About Katie Lynn Swiatek

Educational Background

I earned my B.A. in Studio Art from Florida State University, where I focused on digital media and typography as well as completing minors in both Art History and Communications. After taking a few years off from school, I am currently working towards my M.A. in Web Design and Online Communication through the University of Florida.

Skills

Adobe Creative Suite, Microsoft Office, HTML and CSS coding languages.

I have a love for training, communications, and designing tools which set teams up for success.

Work History

After working as a graphic designer for Florida State University's student run organizations, I moved to Orlando to work for the Walt Disney Company. While working for Disney, I have become a location trainer and Food and BEverage Coordinator, where I now design materials to help communicate updates around our location.
